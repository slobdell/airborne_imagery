Airborne Imagery

Serving the greater Los Angeles area and abroad, Airborne Imagery is an aerial photography company started in associated with EBlimp.com

In addition to providing unique aerial perspectives with helicopter drones and unique to our company as a blimp manufacturer, we are capable of providing services using a stable dirigible for cases in which prolonged air time is preferable or in public venues where safety is required.

While we ensure that the pictures that we take are in public venues and that we are in compliance with all US and international laws as applicable, we understand the privacy concerns associated with drones and are more than willing to remove any photos that may create a perception of infringed privacy.
